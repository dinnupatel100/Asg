Q3. How to combine two commits into one commit? For Example, you have 4 different commits C1, C2, C3, and  C4 and you to combine C2 and C3 into one single commit.
->

"git rebase -i HEAD~2" command is used to combine two commit into one commit and the process is known as squash.

Suppose we have 4 commits C1, C2, C3, and  C4 and you have to combine C2 and C3 into one single commit.
 Step 1 : use command "git rebase -i HEAD~4" 
 Step 2 : replace "pick" with "s" in front of C3 and save it to combine C2 and C3 commit.
